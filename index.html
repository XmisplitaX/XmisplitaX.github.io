if you have entered here, you may know what is territorial.io. However, if you are new and want to learn about it you should know several things.

¿what is territorial.io?

Territorial.io is a videogame where the objetive is to gain the most territory posible managing your troops and making adventage between your opponesnts. It has multiplayer and 1 player mode to practice or just making rpg under your imagination

SPAWNING

The spawn you chose can decide your whole game, normally you spawn where there is more free territoy, that is the neutral territory where its only needed one troop to get one pixel of territory, like 0 density, but this is too advance, so just focus to land on a place where there is the most free territory posible, in team games this changes a little bit cause everyone will want to go to that spawn and the free territory you get probably will be reduced and also more ppl is aviable to attack you. Also try to spawn in a place where bots presece is high, botys are the players with underlined name thath recive less troups and are normally easier to attack. INFINITE EXPANSION/END OPENING

Once you ended opening its time to take the free land left, for this we normaly say to just atact acording you never end of attacking, however i made myself a trick to have a good infinite expansion. it consist on attacking with a 30% troops 2 or 3 times per cycle depending on the position. obviously you can make 50% or other strats that you feel more confortable.

MICRO PHASE

Micro phase is the most important part on 1v1, however on team games is not decisive at all, this phase consist on attacking the bots, if you wonder what bost are, they are the players with underlined name that recive less troops and indeed are easier to kill. Normaly to face this phase we attack bots with 8-11% depending of our troops and in team games do not attack more than 3 bots at the same time unless you know how to manage.

MIDDLE-END GAME

Middle-end game is the moment of the game after ending the micro till the end of the match, here you must focus on attacking the weakest people without becoming you on the weakest, we can achive this controling our density, that i will explain later what is it. So the objetive is to make good decisions, sometimes you have to allie with other players to defeat a bigger one, the most important thing is your benefit and the game-lobby benefit, if one team is dominating you should focus on that team, also you can get very big on a rivals backline, you can do many things but making good decisions is decisive thats what we call game sense, and the lack of it, SUCKS. You will acquier it with experience.

DENSITY/INTEREST

Density is the amount of troops you have per pixel. This is a complex concept and knowing about it is necesary to know how to use it. When you dont attack, your troops increase, well thats called saving up, when you save up you are increasing your density, which is good, this makes you more difficult to conqueer, in way that your rivals will need more troops to conqueer one of your pixels. Aplying this to the mid game, before attacking someone, look your sourondings because if someones with the same troops but with less density (more territory) will need less troops to be conqueered indeed you'll get more territory using the same amounts of troops.
